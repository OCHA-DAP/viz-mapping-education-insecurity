Mapping Education Insecurity

This visual shows reports of attacks on education in Africa and the Middle East from the Armed Conflict Location & Event Data Project (ACLED) as well as social media posts from Twitter about education insecurity identified by the Artificial Intelligence for Digital Response (AIDR) platform. The blue circles represent the number of tweets about attacks on education; the orange triangles represent verified reports of attacks on education from ACLED.

The OCHA Centre for Humanitarian Data created this visual in partnership with the Education Above All Foundation and the Qatar Computing Research Institute to increase access to timely data about education insecurity.

Note: due to a technical issue, Twitter data was collected only in English during April 2020.
